Project 1 - Correlational Analysis of Cardiac Health Metrics

Github

The objective of this study was to analyze a dataset of 270,000 patients with reported heart disease to identify correlations between various metrics and cardiac health. Through structured data analysis, key demographic and behavioral factors are investigated to uncover potential risk contributors. This research also served as an illustration of practical application of SQL and data visualization techniques (created Tableau Dashboard in correlation), advancing data analysis skills in the field of cardiovascular health.
